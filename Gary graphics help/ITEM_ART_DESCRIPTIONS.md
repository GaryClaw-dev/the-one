# Item Art Descriptions — 32x32 Pixel Icons

**Art Style:** Thronefall-inspired. Clean 2D, simple silhouettes, flat shading with 1-2 shadow tones, bold 1-2px dark outlines, minimal internal detail. Warm/muted tones for backgrounds, saturated colors for items. No pixel art grid.

**Rarity Border/Tint Colors:**
- Common: Grey `#B0B0B0`
- Uncommon: Green `#4CBB4C`
- Rare: Blue `#4D9DE0`
- Epic: Purple `#9B59B6`
- Legendary: Gold `#E8C547`

---

## Common Items (Grey tint/border)

### 1. Iron Plate
A front-facing square chest plate, slightly wider at the top, tapering toward the bottom. Flat steel-grey color (`#8A9BA8`) with a single darker grey shadow tone along the left edge and bottom to suggest depth. Simple rivets or a single horizontal band across the middle as the only internal detail.

### 2. Whetstone
A small rectangular grinding stone, oriented at a slight angle. Medium warm grey (`#9E9E9E`) with a lighter highlight stripe across the top face suggesting a smooth, worn surface. The silhouette should be chunky and obvious — clearly a hand stone, not a brick.

### 3. Quiver of Haste
A short, squat quiver (cylinder shape) viewed at a 3/4 angle with 3 arrow fletching fins visible poking out the top. Warm brown leather body (`#8B5E3C`) with a darker brown shadow side. The fletching tips should be a bright accent color like red or yellow to draw the eye upward, suggesting speed.

### 4. Lucky Coin
A round coin face-on, showing a simple star or four-pointed diamond symbol in the center. Warm gold-yellow (`#E8C547`) body with a single darker gold shadow crescent on the lower-left. The coin edge should have a bold dark outline to keep it reading clearly at small size.

### 5. Thorn Ring
A ring viewed at a slight top-down angle so it reads as a clear O shape. The band is dark iron-grey (`#6B6B6B`) with 4-6 sharp spike/thorn protrusions evenly spaced around the outside edge. Keep the spikes short and triangular — bold enough to be readable, not so large they obscure the ring shape.

### 6. Scope Lens
A single circular lens in a simple metal housing frame. The lens itself is a slightly teal-tinted glass color (`#A8D8D8`) with a small bright white glint dot in the upper-right. The metal rim is dark grey, and a subtle crosshair or center dot inside the lens conveys its precision purpose.

## Uncommon Items (Green tint/border)

### 7. Vampiric Fang
A single large canine tooth (fang), slightly curved, pointed end facing down. Ivory-white (`#F0EAD6`) with a pale shadow tone on the left side. A thin drip of deep red blood (`#8B0000`) trails down from the tip, which is the primary visual accent keeping the item readable and thematic.

### 8. Serrated Blade
A short, wide dagger blade (no handle) with a visibly jagged/serrated lower edge. The flat of the blade is a cold steel-blue grey (`#7A92A8`), with a bright highlight stripe running down the center spine. The serrated teeth along the bottom edge should be bold and irregular — that silhouette is the whole identity of this item.

### 9. Blast Powder
A small round pouch/bag tied at the top, with a fuse sticking out from the knot. The bag is a rough dark tan (`#8B7355`) with a shadow side. A small burst of orange-yellow sparks or a lit flame sits at the tip of the fuse as the key accent detail, clearly communicating explosive intent.

### 10. Chain Links
Two or three interlocking chain rings, viewed straight-on so the interlocking is obvious. The rings are flat steel-grey (`#8A9BA8`) with a darker shadow on the inner curve of each link. The links should be thick and heavy-looking — these are armor-weight chains, not delicate jewelry.

### 11. Repeating Mechanism
A gear or cog shape (6-8 teeth) with a smaller gear overlapping it in the lower-right corner, suggesting a clockwork mechanism. The gears are warm brass/copper toned (`#B87333`) with darker brown shadow tones on the teeth. A small dot at the center axle of the large gear adds mechanical detail without cluttering the icon.

### 12. Fire Arrow
A single arrow pointing diagonally (lower-left to upper-right), with an active flame at the tip replacing the arrowhead. The shaft is medium brown wood, the fletching is white or grey. The flame is the focal point — bright orange-yellow core (`#FF8C00`) fading to red at the edges, clearly burning and dynamic.

## Rare Items (Blue tint/border)

### 13. Phasing Bolt
A crossbow bolt or thick arrow shape, but rendered with a ghostly, semi-transparent quality. The body is a pale icy blue-white (`#A8D4F5`) with the trailing half fading into translucency, as if phasing out of reality. A soft blue glow aura surrounds the bolt's outline to reinforce the ethereal, piercing nature.

### 14. Berserker's Gauntlet
A single armored gauntlet (fist closed, facing forward), with 3-4 short angular spikes protruding from the knuckles. The armor plates are dark iron (`#4A4A4A`) with a warm red-orange accent (`#C0392B`) on the spikes and knuckle edges, suggesting aggression and power. Bold and chunky silhouette — this reads as a weapon as much as armor.

### 15. Fortified Bastion
A wide, squat kite shield viewed face-on. Heavy dark grey steel (`#5A6A7A`) body with a thick border line and a single bold cross or diamond motif embossed in the center in a slightly lighter grey. The sheer width and mass of the silhouette communicates defense — make it feel immovable.

### 16. Sniper's Mark
A circular target reticle — a ring with a crosshair (4 lines extending from center to edge) and a small dot at the exact center. The ring and lines are a crisp red (`#E74C3C`) on a very dark, near-black background circle. A faint outer glow in red gives it a laser-sight quality, emphasizing precision and threat.

### 17. Shockwave Emitter
A small rounded device or orb with concentric arc lines radiating outward from it on one side, like a sonar pulse or shockwave burst. The device body is dark metal grey, and the wave arcs are bright electric blue (`#4D9DE0`) getting lighter and thinner as they expand outward. The asymmetric burst shape makes the directional energy feel dynamic.

### 18. Chrono Gear
A clock face merged with a large gear — a circle with gear teeth around the edge and clock hands visible inside. The gear teeth are gold-brass (`#E8C547`) and the clock face interior is a deep navy blue (`#1A2A4A`) with bright white clock hands. The contrast between the warm gear and cool clock face signals the time-manipulation theme.

## Rare Cursed Items (Blue border with red/dark accents)

### 19. Bloodprice Amulet
A teardrop or shield-shaped amulet pendant with a dark red gemstone (`#8B0000`) set in a jagged, cracked metal setting. The metal frame is dark iron with blue rare-rarity undertones, but cracked lines radiate from the gem suggesting corruption. A faint deep red glow emanates from the gem — the power is real, but the cost is visible in the damage to the item itself.

### 20. Parasite Heart
An anatomical heart shape, but visibly alien — asymmetrical, with 2-3 thin tendril-like roots or veins wrapping around the outside. The heart body is a sickly dark red-purple (`#7B2D8B`) with a pulsing bright magenta-pink center glow (`#E040FB`). The tendrils are near-black and organic-looking, making it immediately feel like a living parasite rather than a healthy organ.

## Epic Items (Purple tint/border)

### 21. Deathdealer's Crest
A skull face (front-on, simplified) set inside or framed by an upward-pointing crest or badge shape. The skull is flat white-grey with stark black shadow sockets, and the crest frame is deep purple-black (`#2C1654`). A thin purple glow outlines the whole badge shape, and a small gold accent — a crown or star — sits above the skull to distinguish it as elite.

### 22. Aegis of Eternity
A large ornate shield, face-on, with decorative flourishes — a border pattern of small geometric shapes and a central emblem (sun, eye, or rune). The main body is a rich royal blue (`#2E4A7A`) with gold (`#E8C547`) decorative trim and emblem. The overall silhouette is larger and more imposing than the Rare shield, and the gold detail quantity clearly communicates legendary craftsmanship.

### 23. Gatling Core
A cluster of 3-4 small cylindrical barrels arranged in a circular bundle, viewed from the front (barrel ends facing the viewer), with a larger mechanical ring clamping them together. The barrel ends are dark grey circles, and the outer ring is warm copper-bronze (`#B87333`). A faint motion blur or rotation lines around the outer ring suggests it is already spinning.

### 24. Nova Engine
A smooth rounded orb/core shape with visible internal energy — cracks or seams on the surface glow with intense bright energy. The outer shell is dark charcoal (`#2C2C2C`) with purple-tinted seams, and the glow emanating from the cracks is a vivid blue-white (`#80D4FF`) with a purple outer haze. The contained-explosion quality should feel like enormous energy barely being held in check.

---

## Epic Cursed Items (Purple border with red/dark accents)

### 25. Glass Cannon Prism
A large triangular crystal prism shape with highly visible crack lines running through it. The crystal is a deep violet-purple (`#7B2FBE`) with bright refracted light colors (a thin rainbow spectrum) visible inside, but the cracks are stark black and dominant. The combination of beautiful inner light and severe structural damage visually tells the story of immense power with a fatal vulnerability.

---

## Legendary Items (Gold tint/border with glow)

### 26. Soul Reaper
A large crescent scythe blade (just the blade, no full pole — too small for that), curving from lower-left to upper-right with the sharp inner edge at the bottom. The blade is a spectral pale blue-white (`#C8E8FF`) that fades to near-transparent at the tips, as if it is made of solidified energy. A vivid gold glow (`#E8C547`) halos the entire blade edge, and wisps of purple-white soul energy trail behind the curve — this item should feel like the most visually striking icon on the whole screen.
