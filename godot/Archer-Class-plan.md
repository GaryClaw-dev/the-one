# Archer Class Evolution Plan

## Class Progression Path
**Noob (1-4)** → **Slingshot (5-14)** → **Archer (15-24)** → **Gunner (25+)**

## Slingshot (Level 5 Base Class)

### Identity
- Entry-level ranged attacker
- Learning precision and timing
- Sets foundation for Archer playstyle

### Base Stats (vs Noob)
- Attack Speed: +10%
- Projectile Speed: +20%
- Crit Chance: +5%

### Starting Ability Pool
Uses current archer ability pool (12 abilities) designed for levels 5-14

## Archer Evolution (Level 15)

### Identity
**Precision marksman with burst potential**

### Evolution Requirements
- Level 15
- Currently playing as Slingshot class

### Base Stat Changes
- Attack Speed: +20%
- Crit Chance: +10%  
- Projectile Speed: +30%
- Max Health: +20%

### New Passive Mechanics

#### 1. Aimed Shot (Passive)
- Every 5th attack is a guaranteed critical hit
- Aimed shots pierce +2 additional enemies
- Visual: Glowing arrow charge-up on 4th shot

#### 2. Wind Guidance (Passive)
- All projectiles curve slightly toward nearest enemy
- +15% accuracy (hidden stat)
- Missed shots have 30% chance to loop back

### New Active Ability

#### Focus Mode (10s cooldown)
- Duration: 3 seconds
- Effects:
  - Next 3 attacks deal 300% damage
  - Time slows by 50% while aiming
  - Perfect accuracy (cannot miss)
  - Shots leave wind trails

### Expanded Ability Pool

#### Headshot (Epic)
- Level 1: Critical hits deal +50% damage
- Level 2: Critical hits deal +100% damage
- Level 3: Critical hits deal +150% damage
- Level 4: Critical hits deal +200% damage
- Level 5: Critical hits deal +250% damage and execute enemies below 20% HP

#### Reload Discipline (Rare)
- Level 1: Every kill resets aimed shot counter
- Level 2: Every kill grants +15% attack speed for 2s
- Level 3: Every kill resets counter and +25% attack speed for 3s
- Level 4: Every kill resets counter and next shot pierces all
- Level 5: Every kill triggers an instant aimed shot

#### Archer's Tempo (Rare)
- Level 1: Attack speed increases by 10% per second (max +50%)
- Level 2: Attack speed increases by 15% per second (max +75%)
- Level 3: Attack speed increases by 20% per second (max +100%)
- Level 4: Max tempo grants +25% crit chance
- Level 5: Max tempo shots pierce and bounce once

## Gunner Evolution (Level 25)

### Identity
**Overwhelming firepower and area control specialist**

### Evolution Requirements
- Level 25
- Currently playing as Archer class

### Base Stat Changes (from Archer)
- Attack Speed: +50% (total +80% from base)
- Projectile Count: +1
- AOE Radius: +50 on all attacks
- Move Speed: -10% (heavy weapons)
- Max Health: +50%

### New Passive Mechanics

#### 1. Bullet Hell (Passive)
- All attacks fire in a 30° cone spread
- Each projectile can hit multiple enemies
- Projectiles penetrate based on damage dealt

#### 2. Explosive Rounds (Passive)
- 25% chance for any projectile to explode on impact
- Explosion damage: 50% of projectile damage
- Explosions can chain to nearby enemies (50% chance)
- Creates visual shockwaves

### New Active Ability

#### Suppressing Fire (15s cooldown)
- Channel Duration: 3 seconds
- Effects:
  - +300% attack speed while channeling
  - Creates a 120° kill zone in front
  - Pushback increased by 200%
  - Immune to knockback while channeling
  - Screen shake and muzzle flash effects

### Exclusive Gunner Abilities

#### Ricochet (Epic)
- Level 1: Projectiles bounce 2 times
- Level 2: Projectiles bounce 3 times (+10% damage per bounce)
- Level 3: Projectiles bounce 4 times (+15% damage per bounce)
- Level 4: Projectiles bounce 5 times (+20% damage per bounce)
- Level 5: Bounces seek nearest enemy and can hit same enemy

#### Incendiary Clips (Rare)
- Level 1: 20% chance to ignite (10 DPS for 3s)
- Level 2: 30% chance to ignite (20 DPS for 3s)
- Level 3: 40% chance to ignite (30 DPS for 4s)
- Level 4: 50% chance to ignite, spreads on death
- Level 5: All shots ignite, creates fire pools on death

#### Heavy Ordnance (Legendary)
- Level 1: Every 50 kills: orbital strike (500% damage)
- Level 2: Every 40 kills: orbital strike (750% damage)
- Level 3: Every 30 kills: orbital strike (1000% damage)
- Level 4: Every 20 kills: orbital strike with stun
- Level 5: Every 10 kills: carpet bombing run

#### Gunslinger (Legendary)
- Level 1: Dual wield - duplicate 25% of attacks
- Level 2: Dual wield - duplicate 40% of attacks
- Level 3: Dual wield - duplicate 60% of attacks
- Level 4: Dual wield - duplicate 80% of attacks
- Level 5: True dual wield - all attacks fire twice

## Visual/Audio Design

### Slingshot
- Simple leather sling weapon
- Wooden projectiles
- Soft "thwip" sound effects

### Archer
- Elegant longbow
- Glowing arrow tips
- Wind particle effects on shots
- Satisfying "twang" on aimed shots
- Focus Mode: Time dilation audio effect

### Gunner
- Dual pistols/hand cannons
- Muzzle flash effects
- Shell casing particles
- Explosive impact effects
- Suppressing Fire: Rapid fire audio loop
- Screen shake on Heavy Ordnance

## Balance Philosophy

### Power Progression
- Slingshot: 100% baseline power
- Archer: 200% power spike (+100%)
- Gunner: 350% power spike (+150%)

### Difficulty Scaling
- Enemies should feel challenging until evolution
- Each evolution should provide immediate power fantasy
- New mechanics should synergize with existing abilities

### Build Diversity
- Precision builds: Headshot + Aimed Shot synergy
- Speed builds: Tempo + Reload Discipline
- AoE builds: Explosive + Ricochet for Gunner
- Hybrid builds viable with proper stat balance
