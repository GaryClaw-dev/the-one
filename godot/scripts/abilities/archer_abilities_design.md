# Archer Ability Pool Design

## Design Philosophy
- Early abilities give small % increases that compound dramatically
- Later abilities multiply these gains
- Synergistic combinations lead to overpowered builds
- Each ability has 5 levels, with exponential value increases

## Archer-Specific Abilities

### 1. **Steady Aim** (Common)
- Level 1: +5% crit chance
- Level 2: +10% crit chance  
- Level 3: +15% crit chance
- Level 4: +25% crit chance
- Level 5: +40% crit chance
*Early game reliability, late game crit machine*

### 2. **Quick Draw** (Common)
- Level 1: +8% attack speed
- Level 2: +16% attack speed
- Level 3: +25% attack speed  
- Level 4: +35% attack speed
- Level 5: +50% attack speed
*Core DPS increase that scales with other multipliers*

### 3. **Eagle Eye** (Common)
- Level 1: +10% attack damage
- Level 2: +20% attack damage
- Level 3: +30% attack damage
- Level 4: +45% attack damage
- Level 5: +65% attack damage
*Base damage scaling*

### 4. **Piercing Shot** (Uncommon)
- Level 1: +1 pierce
- Level 2: +2 pierce
- Level 3: +3 pierce, +10% damage
- Level 4: +4 pierce, +20% damage  
- Level 5: +6 pierce, +40% damage
*Crowd control that scales into damage*

### 5. **Double Nock** (Uncommon)
- Level 1: 15% chance to shoot 2 projectiles
- Level 2: 30% chance to shoot 2 projectiles
- Level 3: 50% chance to shoot 2 projectiles
- Level 4: 75% chance to shoot 2 projectiles
- Level 5: Always shoot 2 projectiles + 20% chance for 3rd
*Multiplicative damage scaling*

### 6. **Lethal Precision** (Uncommon)
- Level 1: +20% crit damage
- Level 2: +40% crit damage
- Level 3: +70% crit damage
- Level 4: +110% crit damage
- Level 5: +170% crit damage
*Synergizes with Steady Aim for massive burst*

### 7. **Wind Arrows** (Rare)
- Level 1: +20% projectile speed
- Level 2: +40% projectile speed, +5% attack speed
- Level 3: +60% projectile speed, +10% attack speed
- Level 4: +80% projectile speed, +15% attack speed
- Level 5: +120% projectile speed, +25% attack speed
*Quality of life that becomes DPS*

### 8. **Hunter's Focus** (Rare)
- Level 1: Every 5th shot deals +50% damage
- Level 2: Every 4th shot deals +100% damage
- Level 3: Every 3rd shot deals +150% damage
- Level 4: Every 3rd shot deals +250% damage
- Level 5: Every 2nd shot deals +400% damage
*Rhythm-based multiplier*

### 9. **Explosive Tips** (Rare)  
- Level 1: 10% chance for AoE on hit (50% damage)
- Level 2: 20% chance for AoE on hit (75% damage)
- Level 3: 30% chance for AoE on hit (100% damage)
- Level 4: 45% chance for AoE on hit (150% damage)
- Level 5: 60% chance for AoE on hit (250% damage)
*Late game screen clear*

### 10. **Archer's Arsenal** (Epic)
- Level 1: +1 projectile every 3 seconds
- Level 2: +1 projectile every 2 seconds
- Level 3: +2 projectiles every 2 seconds
- Level 4: +2 projectiles every 1 second
- Level 5: +3 projectiles every 0.5 seconds
*Exponential projectile scaling*

### 11. **Marked for Death** (Epic)
- Level 1: First hit on enemy: +20% damage taken
- Level 2: First hit on enemy: +40% damage taken
- Level 3: First hit on enemy: +70% damage taken
- Level 4: First hit on enemy: +110% damage taken
- Level 5: First hit on enemy: +170% damage taken
*Boss killer ability*

### 12. **Rain of Arrows** (Legendary)
- Level 1: Every 10 kills: rain of 5 arrows
- Level 2: Every 8 kills: rain of 8 arrows
- Level 3: Every 6 kills: rain of 12 arrows
- Level 4: Every 4 kills: rain of 20 arrows
- Level 5: Every 2 kills: rain of 35 arrows
*Screen wipe potential*

## Scaling Examples

### Early Game (Levels 1-10)
- Steady Aim 1 + Quick Draw 1 = +5% crit, +8% attack speed
- Modest improvements, learning the game

### Mid Game (Levels 15-25)  
- Steady Aim 3 + Lethal Precision 2 + Double Nock 2
- 15% crit × 140% crit damage × 30% double shot
- Starting to feel powerful

### Late Game (Levels 30+)
- Steady Aim 5 + Lethal Precision 5 + Hunter's Focus 4 + Archer's Arsenal 3
- 40% crit × 270% crit damage × every 3rd shot +250% × +2 projectiles/2s
- Absolute destruction

### "Overpowered" Synergies
1. **Crit Build**: Steady Aim + Lethal Precision + Hunter's Focus
2. **Multishot Build**: Double Nock + Piercing Shot + Archer's Arsenal  
3. **AoE Build**: Explosive Tips + Rain of Arrows + high attack speed
4. **Boss Killer**: Marked for Death + all damage multipliers
